What is Hubl?

Hubl is an environmentally-friendly city logistics network which gives far greater flexibility and control to the recipient. We’re a small startup developing a pilot project, but expect to see us operating in earnest soon.

Who benefits?

Everyone! Anyone receiving deliveries will enjoy greater convenience and a better service. And by using electric vehicles, cargo cycles and efficient practices, we’ll reduce congestion and pollution too.
